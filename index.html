Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about badCab

Do I need a squad to play?

Not at all! You can play solo matches and get matched with other individual players based on your skill level. Squads are optional but add a fun team dynamic.

How does the MMR system work?

MMR (Matchmaking Rating) is earned by winning matches and lost by defeats. The system considers opponent strength, match type, and performance. Verified coach training can provide additional MMR boosts.

What are coach boosts?

Verified coaches can provide MMR boosts when they confirm significant skill improvement. This ensures the ranking system reflects real ability rather than just match wins.

Is my court attendance data private?

Yes! Attendance tracking is used only for personal progress insights and optional sharing with your squad/club. Your privacy is always protected.

How do I join a club?

Browse available clubs in the app, check their requirements, and send a join request. Club leaders can approve new members based on skill level or other criteria.
